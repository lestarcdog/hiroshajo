It is the improved version of K MAXXI EXTRA, its most important novelties are the beak covering which can be stepped and the sump. It is usable also with crank-handle motor, but in this case it is advisable to choose long trim motor because the steering lever can hang down into the passenger's cab. For this was made the little sidelong wheel corbel, which contains a little windscreen and there is place for the screen of the fish radar. The 20 cm side heighting can protect well on more wavy waters, but doesn't impede in kuttyogtatás (this is a special Hungarian fishing method). Its inner space is totally empty and flat, it can be equipped with seats. With 50 PS motor it is a sport boat with a huge inner space.
It is suitable for further building of customized type with cabin because the upper edge of the deck ensures the drainage.
