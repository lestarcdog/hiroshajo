Welcome to our site!


The Híröshajó (Private) Limited Company deals with the design and production of catamaran bottomed fibreglass plastic ships, boats and fishing boats. Our products: ships, boats and fishboats are sold under different fancy names such as K mini boat&box, K Maxxi, K Maxxi extra, K Maxxi Joker, K1 fisherman, K Maxxi delfin, K Maxxi cabrio, K2 party, K Maxxi party, K maxxi Delfin II at last but not least K Maxxi XXL.
Beginning with designing throughout production finishing with the final quality control we bear in mind the following criteria. Every hull - whether it's a ship supplied with an impressive engine, or a fishing boat equipped with bench seats for several people or a tiny boat for one or two persons; has to meet all the security requirements at the highest level. Its construction should make it suitable for enjoying any kind of activity as they all have to satisfy different requirements when used for sports, or rawing or even several day fishing tours. Different criteria must be met either in the case of a tourist craft, a pleasure craft or a fishing bout used for an overnight tour.

Other important criteria is that materials and technologies used in the course of manufacture should result in long-lifetime and sturdy structure which properly used and with minimal maintenance - that is regular cleaning - will definitely serve its owner for a long time.

If you are looking for a ship, a boat, or perhaps a fishing boat giving value for money, and at a reasonable price or are just interested in our products, you can find a lot of photos and answers to all your questions on the following pages. But of course, I am at your service either personally or on the phone.
