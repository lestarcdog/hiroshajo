Which one should I choose?

I try to summarise in some sentences what kind of questions raise mostly while choosing the type of a boat. The first one: with or without licence? You can drive any boat without little ship licence up to 10 HP/PS motor capacity. With 5 HP/PS you can reach 10 km/h speed which is suitable e.g. for River Tisza, Tisza Lake and the Adriatic.

For travelling e.g. on the Danube - because of its stronger current - you have to choose a stronger engine. Above 25 HP/PS you have to build a motor fixing element in the boat, to which you have to truss the upper fastening screws of your engine. Above 25 HP/PS you will have to choose long boots engine and a boat with extra strenghtening. in addition you will need a wheel and a remote control. From 2014 all the standard boats get an extra crackproof layer which is perfectly fine up to 25 HP/PS engines. Above 25 HP/PS engines it is necessary to strenghten with an extra layer the sliding surface in order to prevent the resonance effect and the damages to the boat.
